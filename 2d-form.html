Vital to the education of an industrial designer or a graphic designer is the ability to make an informed, deliberate, and appropriate visual decision. Students in their first semester of study in the School of Design are required to take DES 120 2D Form Studio in which they are guided through a series of exercises intended to sensitize their eyes to know what to do with the most simple and basic design elements.

During the course of the semester, approximately three class periods were devoted to work on each phase of the exercises, one building upon the experiences and discoveries of the previous one in an 8 step process.
